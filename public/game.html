    How to Play

    Welcome to Lost in the Dark, a maze exploration game where danger lurks in the shadows. Your goal is to find the exit while avoiding enemies and managing your light source.

    Controls

    • Use the arrow keys to move your character.

    Objectives

    • Explore the maze and locate the exit.
    • Collect batteries to keep your flashlight active.
    • Avoid ghosts - getting caught means game over!

    Tips

    • Stay alert - some enemies follow unpredictable paths.
